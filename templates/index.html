About The Product

The acoustic extinguisher works by using sound waves—a type of pressure wave—to push oxygen away from the source of a flame and spread it over a larger surface area. These actions break the fire combustion triangle made up of heat, fuel, and oxygen, the three elements required for a fire to burn.

Frequencies Used

35-200 Hz

Fire Control

Can Control Fires arising from fuels:
Thinner, Gasoline, Kerosene, LPG

FAQ

Commonly Asked Questions about Acoustic Fire Extinguisher.

  • How does the Acoustic Fire Extinguisher work?

    Since sound waves can move oxygen and fire through pressure from their vibrations, this specific frequency of sound wave works to separate the flame molecules from the surrounding oxygen, effectively starving the fire and snuffing out the flame.

  • What are the benefits of acoustic fire extinguisher over no-rmal fire extinguisher?

    ADVANTAGES

    1. There is no residue compared to chemical fire Extinguisher.

    2. Nontoxic to the environment as well as human.

    3. No damage to nearby equipment.

  • Is it needed to be refilled like normal extinguishers?

    It does not need to be refilled. It can also be reused after using it.

  • Is the frequency used safe for humans?

    Yes. The frequency used is in the range of audible frequency range for humans.
